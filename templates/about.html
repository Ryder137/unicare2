About UNICARE

Your Trusted Partner in Mental Health & Emotional Wellbeing

Dedicated to supporting students through their mental health journey with compassionate care, evidence-based tools, and innovative technology. We're here to help you thrive, not just survive.

Our Mission & Vision

UNICARE is dedicated to revolutionizing student mental health support through innovative technology and compassionate care. Our mission is to make mental health resources accessible, engaging, and effective for every student, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, UNICARE was born from a simple yet powerful idea: every student deserves access to quality mental health support. Our team of psychologists, developers, and designers came together to create a platform that bridges the gap between students and mental health resources.

What started as a university project has grown into a comprehensive mental health ecosystem, helping thousands of students navigate their emotional well-being journey.
