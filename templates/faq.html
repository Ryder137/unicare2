Getting Started

How do I get started with UNICARE?

Simply register for an account and complete your first mood assessment. Our chatbot will be ready to support you right away!

Yes, UNICARE is completely free for all students. We're here to support you!

Yes, we take your privacy seriously. All your data is encrypted and stored securely.

We offer emotional support through our AI chatbot, mood tracking, and personalized recommendations.

We recommend checking in daily for the best results, but use it whenever you need support.
